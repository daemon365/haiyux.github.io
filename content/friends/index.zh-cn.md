{{< admonition tip "交换友情链接" >}}
您可以把本网站添加到您的友链，并将您的网站信息在下方评论，格式如下：

```yaml
- nickname: <your nickname>
  avatar: <your avatar>
  url: <your site link>
  description: <description of your site>
```

example:

```yaml
- nickname: haiyux
  avatar: https://haiyux.cc/avatar.png
  url: https://haiyux.cc
  description: haiyux's blog
```

{{< /admonition >}}
